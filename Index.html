Travel Guides & Tips

Best Time to Visit

November to May offers the best weather with minimal rainfall. Peak season is December to February with perfect conditions.

Getting to Palawan

Fly into Puerto Princesa Airport or Busuanga Airport (for Coron). Direct flights available from Manila and Cebu.

What to Pack

Bring reef-safe sunscreen, waterproof bags, snorkeling gear, light clothing, and comfortable walking shoes.

Budget Tips

Book accommodations in advance, join group tours, eat at local eateries, and consider island hopping packages for better deals.

Eco-Tourism Guidelines

Respect marine life, don't touch corals, use reef-safe products, and follow Leave No Trace principles to preserve Palawan's beauty.
